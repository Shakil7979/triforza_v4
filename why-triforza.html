Why TriForza?

Digital Transformation

  • We deliver digital transformation through online tenant engagement and automation for dental benefit companies.

Customer Obsessed

  • We take a customer-first approach in developing our digital experiences and constantly enhance our products based on user feedback.

Dental Benefit Expertise

  • TriForza was created out of Delta Dental Washington and technology leaders from the dental benefits industry, drawing on 70 years of dental benefit management and innovation.

AI-Forward Solutions

  • Our development team is continually looking for areas in benefit management processes where AI can drive greater accuracy, cost savings, or scale. We work with multiple AI vendors to leverage a broad set of expertise, as well as our own proprietary benefit management AI algorithms.

Speed-to-Market

  • Empowered by our history and experience in benefit management technology, we can help you deploy your solution in significantly less time than building it yourself or going direct with other vendors.
