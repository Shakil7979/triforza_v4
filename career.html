Join Our Team

At TriForza, we develop innovative technology for benefit management companies that drives digital transformation and advances the experiences of their customers and employees. We believe in the connection between good oral health and overall health and that everyone deserves a healthy smile. Our technology helps promote access to affordable and quality care for everyone – with no one left behind.

We work hard to leverage technology to improve the patient experience for a better future. We hire employees who are driven by a passion for helping others, to create a future where good health and access to care are available to everyone.

EXPLORE JOBS AT TRIFORZA

Benefits

We offer an affordable and comprehensive traditional benefits package that covers medical, dental, and vision. We also provide cutting edge benefits that promote physical and emotional wellbeing, encourage community engagement, and support career development.

Health Benefits

  • We offer medical, dental, and vision coverage with no additional cost for children/dependents. We also provide company base Life Insurance, Short-Term Disability, and Long-Term Disability Coverage.

Paid Time Off

  • Generous paid time off for vacation, illness, holidays, volunteerism, preventive care visits, and floating holidays. The variety of paid time off options allow employees to take days to volunteer in communities, get their preventive care completed, and celebrate holidays that are meaningful to them that may not be company-wide office closures.

Wellness

  • We encourage employee wellbeing through our wellness program. We will reimburse up to $480/annually for wellness related expenses (gym memberships, gardening, equipment purchases, and more).

Ongoing Education

  • Career Development support through LinkedIn Learning, tuition reimbursement and more. All employees have access to LinkedIn Learning and specially curated courses by our L&D Team. Additionally, as an employee you will have access to up to $5250/year in tuition reimbursement and a student loan repayment program that pays up to $150/month and/or up to a $10,000 lifetime max to assist with student debt.

Plan for the Future

  • All employees are eligible to participate in the company 401(k) plan beginning from their first day of employment. For employees with an annualized base salary over $100,000, we offer a full match on your pre-tax and/or Roth contributions up to an 8% total match. For employees with an annualized base salary of $100,000.00 or below, we match your pre-tax and/or Roth deferral plus an additional 4%, up to a combined 12% total match. There is a three-year vesting schedule on the company match. 

Pay It Forward

  • We firmly believe in giving back to our communities. In addition to each employee having access to volunteer hours throughout the year, we match employee donations to nonprofit organizations up to $750 per year. We also have an annual giving campaign where we match even more.
